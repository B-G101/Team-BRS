Instructions

Welcome to the song association game! This is the page where we give you all the directions on how to play this fantabulous musical game that rapid fire tests your pop song musical knowledge! First, select your game mode (easy - 25 sec, medium - 15 sec, hard - 8 sec). Once you click that button, it will lead you to the game where youa ready to play! Your goal? It's to finish the lyric of the song given. You can keep playing until you run out of songs, but be careful! Your progress doesn't get saved so if you are happy with you score, screenshot it and share it with some friends :) Good luck!
